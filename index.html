About Us

NAQSH Factory for Wooden and Steel Furniture is a fullservice furnishing firm, based in Dammam- Saudi Arabia, founded in 1426H.

It follows NAQSH Contracting Company and has accomplished 18 years of excellence, experience, high quality and productivity. In NAQSH Factory we are proud of our Saudi nationality and our High-quality products.
